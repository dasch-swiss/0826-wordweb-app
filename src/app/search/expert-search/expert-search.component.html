Pending further funding, WordWeb/IDEM 2.0 will offer an expert search option with the following features:
  • Searches for complex research questions such as "Show all passages which quote Doctor Faustus" or "Show all passages in which John Marston quotes tragedies".
  • Innovative visualizations of search results
  • Additional search parameters including date of first performance, author's gender, research field and formal classification of quoted phrases and expressions.

Pending further funding, WordWeb/IDEM 2.0 will offer an expert search option with additional search parameters and further search options.
New search parameters will include:
  • Date of first performance
  • Actors of the first performance if known
  • Date of first publication if known
  • Subject of nonfiction texts
  • Research field
  • Formal class of Lexias
  • Images
  • Contributor (researchers who edited WordWeb/IDEM entries)
    • Additional features will include visualizations options for search results and, most importantly, the option to enter complex research questions such as "Show all passages which quote Doctor Faustus" or "Show all passages in which John Marston quotes tragedies".

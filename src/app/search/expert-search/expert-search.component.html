Pending further funding, WordWeb/IDEM 2.0 will offer an expert search option with additional search parameters and further search options.
New search parameters will include:
  • Contributor who edited and uploaded a WordWeb/IDEM entry
  • Date of first performance of a play
  • Date of first publication in print
  • Gender of the author
  • Lexia category so that users can filter out quoted names, key words, phrases or longer passages
  • Titles and authors of publications which we have used to find quotations, for example annotated editions or articles and books from various research fields.
  • Research field in which a quotation was first identified (Shakespeare studies, Classical studies etc.)
    • Additional features will include visualizations options for search results and, most importantly, the option to enter complex research questions such as "Show all passages which quote Doctor Faustus" or "Show all passages in which John Marston quotes tragedies".
